# Single Responsibility Principle or SRP

## A class must have only one responsibility, that is one and only job to perform and to perform it well and therefore have only one reason to change

A large organization may have different teams or departments, say, a human resources department, management department, engineering department, etc.. Each one of these departments focus soley on one task, the task they were trained for and what they are good at. Nothing more. An HR member won't be (and shouldn't be) seen performing tasks that normally correspond to an engineer.
